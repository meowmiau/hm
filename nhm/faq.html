During Program

What happens during Phase 1 (the first 4 month)?

This is a detail of the program. You’ll receive Hackmind Talent Fund of €2,000/month in Phase 1.

What happens after Phase 1?

Hackmind Talent Fund ends after 4 month. If your project is selected to enter Phase 2, your team will receive €100,000 investment for a portion of future token to launch the ICO.

Are all founders guaranteed to enter Phase 2?

Unfortunately not all founders will enter Phase 2. You’ll be noticed during the last week of Phase 2 whether your project is selected.

What happens if I don’t build a successful startup at the end of the program?

At hackmind we believe failure sucks – especially if you call it failure. Contrarily, if you’re afraid to fail, then you’ll never succeed. We view failure as ‘exponential learning.’ Success is wonderful, and our goal is to provide all of the necessary tools to turn your obsession into a world changing company. However, If you complete the program without building a successful company, you still have a number of options. You could join another startup from our program or we help you develop your career elsewhere. Despite the outcome, you’ll always be part of the hackmind family.

Can I incorporate the company outside Germany?

Yes. In the past, we have helped our founders incorporate companies in the U.S. etc.

Does my company need to stay in Germany after the program?

No. We encourage our participants to stay in Germany, but it is not compulsory. If basing your project elsewhere will get you the highest growth, we support the decision.

How much do you invest?

We invest €2,000/month in return for 3.5% of any ideas that you developed in the Phase 1. If you enter Phase 2, we will invest €100,000 for a portion of your future token.
